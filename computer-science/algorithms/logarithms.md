# Logarithms

A **Logarithm** is 

> A quantity representing the power to which a fixed number (the base) must be raised to produce a given number.


- a mathematical expression
- inverse to exponents




## How to calculate

$$log_{10}1000 = 3$$ which means that $$10^3 = 1000$$
